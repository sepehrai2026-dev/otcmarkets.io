How to choose an OTC trading desk

Why trade OTC?

OTC desks let you execute large trades without moving the market. Instead of placing orders on a public exchange — where your size signals intent and causes slippage — an OTC desk matches you with private liquidity at a fixed price. This is how institutions, funds, and treasuries move crypto.

Key factors to evaluate

Look at minimum trade sizes, supported assets, settlement speed, and counterparty risk. The best desks offer 24/7 coverage, competitive spreads, and settlement within hours. Regulatory compliance and custody solutions also matter — especially for institutional mandates.

Spreads and pricing

OTC spreads vary by asset, size, and market conditions. Most desks offer tighter spreads for larger trades and major assets like BTC and ETH. Always request quotes from multiple desks to ensure you're getting competitive pricing.

Settlement and custody

Settlement typically occurs same-day or next-day. Some desks offer integrated custody through partners like Fireblocks or BitGo, while others settle to your own wallet. Understand the settlement process and counterparty risk before committing capital.
